## Safe ender

- 236.M - whenever you are using triple attack, if it is blocked, always use this at the end to make sure you're safe. you still lose your turn.

## Pokes/Blockstrings/Frametraps/+Frames

- f.M > 
  - 214.L - true block string (special can't be dodged or reversal)
  - 236.L - frametrap, on KD can run up and do meaty 66L
- f.H >
  - 236.L - frametrap, on KD can run up and do meaty 66L
  - 214.L - true block string. at max range opponent might cause a spacing trap letting you punish with f.M in case opponent whiffs
  - 5H - frametrap. juggles in corner for c.M follow up
    - c.MXX > 623.L - corner juggle follow-up
- 66L >
  - corner carry combo
  - walk up and grab - also beats dodge
- 236.H - +Frames

## Mixups

- +Frames into strike/throw

## Anti-Air

- 2H

## Cross-up

- j.U
  - c.LXX - only way to combo if j.U hit
  - c.MXX - true blockstring
  - c.HXX - frametrap if j.U blocked high/early

## Corner-Carry/Punish combo

- c.MXX > 
  - 214.M - use the time of the triple attack to confirm your hit, even the opponent blocked it use safe ender instead
    - 236.L - will hit meaty on wake up
  - 214.U, c.HXX > 236.H - spend 50% meter for more damage and carry
  - super - if you confirmed you are hitting your opponent and have super you can always combo into super after triple attack

## Corner combo

- c.MXX > 214.H > c.MXX >
  - super - if you have it
  - 623.L - if you don't
