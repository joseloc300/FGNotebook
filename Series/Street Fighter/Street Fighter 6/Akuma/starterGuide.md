# Starter Guide

## Pokes

- st.HK - forward momentum, +3 on block
- st.HP - good range
- cr.MK - low, DR cancellable

## Anti-airs

- dp.P
- cr.HP - unreliable
- 4.HK - slow

## Hit-Confirms

- st.LP > st.LP > st.LP > dp.HP - 1650 dmg
- cr.MK > DR, st.HP, st.MP > st.MP, dp.HP - 2265 dmg
- st.HK, st.MP > st.MP > qcb.HK - 2790 dmg, safe-jump with j.HP (2-3f gap)
- st.HP > DR, st.HK, st.HP, qcb.HK - 2768 dmg, safe-jump with j.HP (2-3f gap)

## Punishes

### Fast (4 frames)

- st.LP, st.MP > st.MP > qcb.HK - 2130 dmg

### Slow

- st.HK, st.HP > qcb.HK - 3040 dmg
- st.HK, st.HP > qcb.LK, dp.HP - 3140 dmg
- st.HK, st.HP > qcb.LK, Lv1 - 3560 dmg
- st.HK, st.HP > qcb.LK, Lv2 - 3920 dmg
  - ... > st.HP > dp.HP - 4230 dmg, only on wall-bounce in corner
- st.HK, st.HP > qcb.LK, Lv3 - 4640 dmg

## DI counter

- st.HK, st.MP > st.MP > qcb.HK - 2790 dmg
- st.HK, st.MP > st.MP, qcb.LK, dp.HP - 2640 dmg
- st.HK, st.MP > st.MP, qcb.LK, Lv1 - 3150 dmg
- st.HK, st.MP > st.MP, qcb.LK, Lv2 - 3610 dmg
  - ... > st.HP > dp.HP - 3840 dmg, only on wall-bounce in corner
- st.HK, st.MP > st.MP, qcb.LK, Lv3 - 4490 dmg

## Wall Crumple (on DI block)

- st.HP, qcb.MK, dp.LP - 2056 dmg

## Wall Splat (on DI hit)

- st.HP, qcb.MK, dp.LP - 2690 dmg
